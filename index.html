Education

The Velammal Vidyalaya, Madurai is committed to the pursuit of excellence in teaching and learning. Individual differences are fostered in a caring environment and students are encouraged to enjoy the wide range of intellectual, cultural, sporting and social opportunities offered by the school. Students are encouraged to care for and respect others and to be socially responsible.

Our holistic approach to education is developing the whole person.By using the best available international resources and standards, the school offers an education that is both different and unique, while preparing the students to meet the global challenges of the future. We believe we offer an excellent model of education one that others will seek to emulate.
